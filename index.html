About PublishIt

PublishIt is a digital knowledge platform created by Probhat Patnaik. The purpose of this platform is to publish meaningful work across data analytics, finance, Power BI, research, photography, and personal writing.

Instead of only showing final outputs, PublishIt will also explain the background, purpose, dataset, methodology, insights, and learning behind each project.
